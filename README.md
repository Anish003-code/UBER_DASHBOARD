# 🚖 Uber Ride Analytics Dashboard

## 📰 Headline
Analyzing ride bookings, cancellations, revenue trends, and customer experience across multiple vehicle categories.

---

## 📌 Short Description
An interactive and data-driven Power BI dashboard designed to analyze ride-booking operations for a platform like Uber.The dashboard provides insights into completed bookings, cancellations, revenue generation, vehicle-wise performance, ride distance trends, and customer/driver satisfaction metrics to support operational and business decision-making.

---

## ⚙️ Tech Stack

- **Power BI Desktop:** Used for creating interactive dashboards and visualizations.
- **Power Query:** Performed data cleaning, transformation, and preprocessing.
- **MySQL:** Used for storing and querying ride-booking datasets.
- **Excel / CSV:** Data source handling and preprocessing.
- **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions):** Created KPIs, calculated measures, and business metrics.
- **GitHub:** Version control and project documentation.
- **File Formats:** `.pbix` for development and `.png` for dashboard preview.

---

## 📊 Data Sources

### Uber Ride Booking Dataset
- Booking details
- Vehicle type data
- Revenue information
- Customer & driver ratings
- Cancellation records
- Distance travelled data

---

## 🔹 Business Problem

Ride-booking companies process thousands of bookings daily, making it difficult to manually monitor operational performance, cancellations, revenue generation, and customer satisfaction.

---

## 🔹 Goal of the Dashboard

- Monitor completed vs cancelled bookings
- Analyze monthly revenue trends
- Identify top-performing vehicle categories
- Track customer and driver satisfaction ratings
- Measure total ride distance covered
